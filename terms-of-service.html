2. Service Description

Provyi is a platform that connects requesters (individuals or businesses seeking surveillance services) with spotters (individuals who perform video recording tasks).

The App allows requesters to:

  • Create surveillance tasks with specific locations and requirements
  • Set payment amounts for completed tasks
  • Review and approve submitted recordings
  • Manage task status and communications

The App allows spotters to:

  • Browse and accept available tasks
  • Navigate to task locations using GPS
  • Record videos according to task specifications
  • Submit recordings for review and payment
